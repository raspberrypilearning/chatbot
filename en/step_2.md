## Your chatbot

--- task ---

Before you start making your chatbot, you need to decide on its personality. __Think about the following questions__:

+ What is their name?
+ Where do they live?
+ Are they happy? Serious? Funny? Shy? Friendly?
+ What do they like and dislike?

--- /task ---
